# Code of Conduct

The walastack-rs project adopts the
[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
as its code of conduct.

All participants — contributors, maintainers, and community members —
are expected to abide by its terms.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers
at **contact@walalabs.tech**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly.

Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and safety of reporters.
